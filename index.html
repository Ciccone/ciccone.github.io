About Myself

My name is Alex Ciccone. I'm a computer engineer with interests in embedded systems, data science, and web development. I received bachelor's degrees in Computer Engineering and East Asian Languages & Literatures (Japanese) from the University of Florida.

Most recently I had the privilege of working as an Applications Engineer with National Instruments (NI) in Austin, Texas. I learned a great deal about delivering comprehensive and personalized front-line technical support across a wide range of hardware and software suites.

Alas, all good things come to an end, and I am currently looking for career opportunities in and around Austin, Texas.
